WINTER IS DESIGNED FOR YOU

Winter is tailored to how you use your kitchen and what smart appliances you have synced to the application. It learns your cooking habits and recommends the ideal recipes based on ingredients you have on hand.

Ever wondered what temperature is the oven or how full is the fridge? With Winter you can easily keep an eye on all your smart appliances in one single app. All you need is a stable Wifi or 3G connection to receive real time updates on the condition of your synced appliances.

Items you place in any smart fridges or pantry synced to Winter are automatically scanned and store into the database to be monitored. You can analysis its properties and know when ingredients are about to expire since database is constantly updated in real time to ensure accuracy.

Save time and money by searching for recipes based on ingredients you already have. Winter’s custom recipe search engine determines what you have in your inventory database then cross search online and through Winter’s own collection of user submitted recipes to recommend the perfect recipe for you. Winter analyses and learn your cooking habit overtime.

Can’t wait for get home and eat after a long day? Preheat your oven before you get home and minimise cooking time. Winter works like a remote for you smart appliances. As long as you have a stable Wifi or 3G connection you can control your appliances through the app anywhere, anytime.
