Frequently Asked Questions

What is Winter?

Winter is a smart kitchen application for monitoring and controlling a series of smart appliances. Some of our features include, monitoring ingredients and recommending recipes based on available ingredients.



How much is Winter?

Winter is expected to be free once available. However this might or might not change in the future.



How do I get Winter?

Currently nowhere but once released you can find Winter in the App Store or Google Play.



When is it coming?

Winter is currently under development and there is no set release date. However you can sign up to receive the latest news and become the first to own Winter.



Do I have to sign up to use Winter?

Yes, Winter is cater to the individual or family so it is required to have an account or more associated with the application.



What appliances is it compatible with?

At the moment it is compatible with Winter’s own smart appliances as well as LG’s smart fridge. We are working on compatibility with more appliances soon.



I have a technical issue, where can I get help?

The best way to get in touch with us is through our form here.



Is it available in my language?

Winter only support English at the moment.



Can I control dragons with Winter?

If you name your appliance "dragon" then yes you certainly can become the mother/father of dragons.
